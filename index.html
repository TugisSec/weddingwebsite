Our Story

We first met on a rainy Tuesday afternoon at a small coffee shop downtown. What started as a chance encounter over spilled coffee turned into hours of conversation, laughter, and an instant connection that neither of us could ignore.

Three years later, James proposed during a sunset walk on the same street where we first met. Now we're celebrating our beautiful journey together as husband and wife.
